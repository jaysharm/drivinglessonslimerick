Learn To Drive in Limerick

At Limerick City's Longest Running Driving School

So you've got a desire to drive, great you are in the right place.

  1. First off, before starting to drive you need to have passed your theory test, there are loads of great books and cds out there, best advice is here – Official Theory Site
  2. Next apply for your driving permit, you can do that here – Apply For Your Permit
  3. Finally, get in contact! ShannonSide Driving School is the number one school in Limerick, get in touch and I'll steer you in the right direction.

Applying for your driving Test

  • Have some lessons! It is now compulsory to have at least 12 hours of lessons completed before you sit your test.
  • Contact the RSA to book your test. Note there are a number of fake websites out there that will charge high fees, don't fall for it and only use the official RSA site – Book Your Test.
  • As the saying goes – fail to prepare, prepare to fail. The test is not easy and you will not pass without the proper tuition and practice, get in touch and keep up the practice between lessons.

Good Luck and Safe Driving!
